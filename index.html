About Japji Sahib

Japji Sahib is the opening composition of the Sri Guru Granth Sahib, composed by Guru Nanak Dev Ji. It is recited as the daily morning prayer of the Sikhs and is considered the essence of the entire Granth Sahib — containing within it the foundational principles, disciplines, and ideals of Gurmat.

The composition consists of a Mool Mantar (Root Principle), an introductory Salok, 38 Pauris (stanzas), and a concluding Salok. Each Pauri builds on the last, guiding the seeker from the nature of Reality toward the method of its realization.

  • Mool Mantar — Root Principle
  • Salok (Opening) — Underlying Reality
  • Pauris 1–38 — The Journey
  • Salok (Closing) — Seal of the Composition
